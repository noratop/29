A sunny day in July // Renovating

THAT HOUSE GOT STYLE.

The kind of style you're willing to create in your own home. The style that doesn't need much more, not too much furniture, some plants and a dog will do! Even better, Limo is matching the wood panel walls. Such a perfect fit!

I thought I had taken a photo of Lemo's head popping out of under the covers of the giant bed in Rob & Cat's bedroom, but I can't seem to get the hold of it. Damn!

We had the most enjoyable day sanding (or taking paint out) on that sunny afternoon, the only one we got, hence it's worth mentioning. And the light was a photographer's delight. Natural light is the best. A real inspiring day, all working at the same time, having a cup of tea and a browny on the porch... because sometimes, I do want to photograph the real REAL, not just the beauty of the adventure and the perfection of the people! Yes, photography can be a bit of a lie (take a look at instagram for example!!). Ain't all the feeds filled with perfection? Perfect dish, flowers, people, feet, beach, dress, holidays... Although there is a lot of perfection and artistic aesthetics in a "real" day too; when you didn't pick your cloth or wear a builder's mask! 

The "real" is an unfinished house, it is grass growing everywhere popping out of the concrete, it is an old shed with mismatched paint and cracks. It is generations of owners and things getting old and ugly. It is Nora sanding with his mask and Nadine trying to get the hell of this paint off the roof while standing on one feet on a plastic chair. And it is Rob & Cat enjoying a short break and well deserved sushi. It is kind of a perfect day and the loveliest moment to share.
