Here we were, in that place we had been thinking about for years: Australia. The great landscapes of my worst nighmare and best daydreaming. The loneliness, redish color and silence. I've read of Tim Winton, that describes Australia in many ways, sounds and animals, all of them spectacular. And in July 2012 I was listening to those crazy sounds at night, scared of birds flying around our heads, of dingos roaming around our car and kangaroos jumping in front of our lights. The dream really! The power of threats and the beauty around us, it is what made us thrive and allowed us to conjure. That's what make happiness in a sense.

Traveling is also the best thing you can do as a photographer, that relentless pursuit of exploration, of the unknown and for whatever reason, of the far away countries. We do that mostly to appreciate when looking back in retrospect and to be able to tell a world everyday different.

These days, July 2014, 2 years later, we end up exploring again, in New Zealand. Taking with us all this AWEsomeness we had in Australia and putting it together in the land of great lakes, mountains, alive volcanoes, and the legendary kiwi kindness. We're slowly getting to a place where we could stay for a while longer, settle, enjoy and repeat. We're not there yet as you will see!
