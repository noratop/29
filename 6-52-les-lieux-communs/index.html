The speech bubble week tour, made people talk and wonder !

Everytime you do something out of the ordinary, something new but not revolutionary - like going to the supermarket with a big cardboard speech bubble - people immediatly react to it, out of curiosity, offence or game. We are completely shaped by the surrending culture, playing again and again with those "lieux communs" or commonplaces ideas that made people we met this week think we were doing an art project, or an instagram contest!

The photoshoot with the sunnies bubble, was a way to remind ourself that we evolve in those commonplaces by doing all year long those same things and having repeated speeches that shape our lives. But still, we can have great fun in the mundane and unremarkable clichés.
