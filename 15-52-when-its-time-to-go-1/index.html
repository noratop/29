I wanted to make a gallery of our best moments in Australia, a fancy conclusion! It would have taken me a few weeks to select, edit and put together all of this. So as much as I love to get back to old (2years old) photos and good memories, I wanted to sum it all up. What was it about those 2 years? Australia, the relax country, coffee addict people, stunning and mesmerizing sceneries... I'd like to write for hours about all that. As it would put everyone to sleep, I chose one road trip, one key moment that translate our foundness of that country and heaps of fun we had there. The DESERT. From Darwin (Northern Territory) to Coober Pedy (South Australia) and a little bit of Broken Hill (New South Wales).

We had some good moments, some epic bbq in the dark with giant frogs and dingos' song. The camera battery ran down while we were at the top of Uluru by ourselves (literaly alone) watching the sun setting on the big rocks of Kata Tjuta... we kept enjoying the moment on top of that sacred stone!!

Remembering those Aussie adventures makes you want to go back on the road again!
BUT, I'm planning on doing a "best of the best" of ALL of our road trip in Australia. A suivre...
